In the heart of Porto Covo village, this is a renovated 2 storey house with a modern take on tradition, where I love to come and spend time with my family and friends. With a fantastic view into the bay and to the coastal wild surfing beaches, this is a 2 bedroom house with a large living area and a fantastic terrace in the back. Connect with nature by adventuring yourself on the walking trails of the Natural Vicentina Route, surfing and enjoying the life of the picturesque village.

The house is split in 2 floors. On the ground floor you'll enjoy of an open plan with a large are of living room, dining room and kitchen, connecting to the fantastic terrace overlooking the bay and in the winter a firewood fireplace. On the top floor we have 2 bedrooms, one double bedroom with a private veranda overlooking the bay and the coast and another bedroom with 2 (+2) single beds - great for single adults or a group of kids - and a bathroom. Overall the house is great to walk to the beach, socialising and enjoying the life in the village without having to drive.
